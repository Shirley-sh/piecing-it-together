Final Project

Apr 21, 2016

For our final project, we are gonna continue to work on our previous fish tank assignment, and we gonna make it in to a orbiting lamp.

There will be a motor placed at the bottom that makes the fish tank orbit. The LED in the middle and the outside track will stay still. There will be a pipe in the middle that let the wires go through and hold the central connection of the fans.
